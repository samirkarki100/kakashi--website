Team 7

Team 7 is one of the most iconic squads from the Hidden Leaf Village in the Naruto series. Originally led by Kakashi Hatake, the team consists of Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno. Together, they embark on numerous missions, face formidable foes, and form a bond that shapes the fate of the ninja world. Their teamwork, growth, and friendship are central themes throughout the series.
